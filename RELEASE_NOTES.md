### High Sierra disc support
Disc Xplorer now reads **High Sierra** discs — the 1986 predecessor to ISO 9660 used by early CD-ROMs (Kodak Photo CD, first-generation titles). These previously failed to open with a parse error.

- Auto-detected from the volume signature and shown as a **High Sierra** filesystem in the sidebar
- Full browsing and extraction, just like ISO 9660 — the format's different directory-record layout (6-byte dates, shifted flags, extra path-table pointers) is handled transparently
